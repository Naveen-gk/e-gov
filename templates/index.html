Discover Tamil Nadu
 Land of Heritage & Wonders

Kanniyakumari

The southernmost tip of India, Kanyakumari is where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet. Famous for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views, the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, and the Thiruvalluvar Statue, it’s a place of spiritual and scenic beauty. With its rich cultural heritage and serene coastal charm, Kanyakumari is a must-visit destination.

Chennai

A vibrant metropolis blending tradition and modernity, Chennai is the cultural and economic hub of Tamil Nadu. Known for its stunning Marina Beach, iconic temples, and rich Carnatic music heritage, the city offers a perfect mix of history and innovation. From its colonial landmarks to its bustling IT corridors, Chennai is a city that never stops evolving
